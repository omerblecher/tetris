Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Tetris app ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive your:

  • Display name
  • Email address
  • Google profile photo URL
  • A unique user ID assigned by Google

We also store your best score in the game.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your name on the leaderboard
  • To save and retrieve your personal best score
  • To authenticate you across sessions

We do not use your information for advertising, analytics, or any other purpose.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase (Firestore). One document per user is stored, containing your display name and best score. Firebase's privacy policy applies: firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties. Your display name and score are visible to other players on the public leaderboard.

5. Sign-In is Optional

You can play the game without signing in. Signing in is only required to submit your score to the leaderboard.

6. Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data, contact us at the email below. We will remove your account data from Firestore within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

9. Contact
